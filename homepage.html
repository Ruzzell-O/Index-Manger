    Welcome to
    Index Manager!

    The Index Manager encompasses the necessary requirements for HRM staff by providing a database displayed by a table that showcases the different applicants they will encode for their organization. Through the use of a form sheet within the program, the HRM staff can easily input each necessary information needed to be provided for their organization alongside the capabilities to modify, profile, and delete their records of applicants.
